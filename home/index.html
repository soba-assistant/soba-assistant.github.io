Welcome to SOBA Assistant

SOBA is an AI-powered plugin that enhances your Eclipse modeling workflow. It uses GPT-based Large Language Models to help you create, refine, and manage models like UML, XMI, BPMN, and Ecore.

Why use SOBA?

  • 💬 Natural language model editing
  • 🌳 Visual tree for GPT suggestions
  • 🧠 Smart assistant inside your IDE
  • 🛠️ Works with multiple metamodels

Start by checking the How to Start or jump directly into the Tutorial.
